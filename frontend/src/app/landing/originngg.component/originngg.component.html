Origin of NGG Plants

NGG Plants - NextGen NurseryThings originated in Kadiyam, East Godavari, a region well known for its long standing nursery cultivation practices.The availability of fertile soil, favourable climatic conditions, and a skilled nursery workforce provided a suitable base for starting an organized plant supply initiative.

The organization was founded in 2020, during a period of increasing infrastructure devolopment and landscaping activities across public and private sectors.

NGG Plants was started to meet the need for good quality plants at reasonable prices for large-scale landscaping and green devolopment projects, where sourcing consistent and affordable plants in bulk was a common challenge

Its establishment was driven by the intent to make dependable plant sourcing more accessible for project requiremnts. This purpose formed the project requirements. This purpose formed the foundation on which the organization was built.
